The Future of Human Cloning

Technological advancements like therapeutic cloning could lead to breakthroughs in organ regeneration, genetic research, and treatments for previously untreatable conditions. According to BMC Medical Ethics (2024), similar to CRISPR technology, human cloning may face initial skepticism but could eventually gain acceptance for its potential to cure genetic diseases.
