Medical Benefits

Therapeutic cloning allows for the production of specific cells for medical use. For example, it enables the generation of organs genetically identical to the patient, reducing the risk of organ rejection and saving countless lives. As GENESIS Fertility & Reproductive Medicine (2024) shows, other reproductive technologies like IVF have been widely accepted, paving the way for therapeutic cloning.

In regenerative medicine, cloning technology could repair or replace damaged tissues, improving the quality of life for patients with severe illnesses. This presents a groundbreaking opportunity for the medical field.
