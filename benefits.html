Medical and Social Benefits

Human cloning provides groundbreaking opportunities in both medicine and society. Therapeutic cloning allows for the production of specific cells for medical use, such as generating organs genetically identical to patients. This eliminates the risk of organ rejection and reduces waiting times for transplants.

Additionally, cloning offers solutions for families who cannot conceive naturally. Like in vitro fertilization (IVF), cloning brings hope to many. According to GENESIS Fertility & Reproductive Medicine (2024), IVF was initially controversial but is now widely accepted. Human cloning can follow this path, benefiting millions while advancing science.
