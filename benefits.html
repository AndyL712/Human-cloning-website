Medical Benefits

Human cloning offers significant medical advancements. Therapeutic cloning could enable the generation of organs genetically identical to patients, reducing the risk of organ rejection and saving countless lives.

Additionally, it presents groundbreaking opportunities in regenerative medicine, helping to repair or replace damaged tissues. As GENESIS Fertility & Reproductive Medicine (2024) pointed out, reproductive technologies like IVF and surrogacy were once controversial but are now widely accepted, paving the way for human cloning to gain similar recognition.
